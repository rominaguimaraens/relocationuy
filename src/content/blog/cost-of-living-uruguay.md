Numbers are only useful when they reflect how people actually live. This guide runs through the budgets we see most often for singles, couples, and families settling into Montevideo’s most popular neighborhoods.

We talk through common surprises like gastos comunes on apartments, seasonal electricity spikes, prepaid health plans, and the price of a weekly produce haul at the feria. Consider it a local friend walking you through their spreadsheets so you can arrive with honest expectations.
